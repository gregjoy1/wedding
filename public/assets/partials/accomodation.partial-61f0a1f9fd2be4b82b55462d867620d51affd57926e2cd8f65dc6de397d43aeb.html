Heather House B&B

Located on the Iffley Road, Heather House B&B is a short 4 minute drive from Hawkwell House Hotel. There are good transport links from the centre of Oxford to Iffley Road with the number 3 bus running every 5 - 10 minutes. For those who prefer walking, the centre of Oxford is around a 20 - 30 minute walk, with fantastic views of the surrounding colleges and the Botanic Gardens.

Manor House Hotel

A 3 minute drive away from the venue is the Manor House Hotel, a Victorian family run hotel. Once again, this is the perfect location for those who want to be close to the action with the added bonus of being within walking distance of the city of Oxford. The number 3 bus will take you straight into the centre, or if you want to explore the more colourful sights of the city, Cowley Centre is also around a 10 minute walk away.
